Do you have a business idea and want to bring it to the next level?

Looking for mentorship and funding opportunities?

Our annual NES Spark Tank X pitching competition organised by NUS Entrepreneurship Society (NES) is the perfect opportunity for you! Selected top 5 finalist teams get to pitch their business ideas to investors and business professionals, standing a chance to walk away with up to $1500 of cash prizes! Each of the top 5 teams will also be awarded with $5000 Amazon Web Services (AWS) credits to kickstart their business ideas!

Additionally, ALL qualified startup teams who sign up will get a guaranteed 1-to-1 mentorship opportunity with startup professionals like founders and VCs through our special Mentorthon segment to get feedback and validation for their early stage start-up ideas and accelarate their success! Selected teams are also entitled to their own booth (worth $150) on event day itself to feature their startups!
